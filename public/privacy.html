01

What we collect

We collect only what we need to run your briefing:

  • Your Canvas domain and Iris User ID
  • Canvas course, assignment, and announcement data synced from your account
  • Your phone number, if you choose SMS delivery
02

How we use it

We use your data solely to generate and send your daily academic briefing. We don’t use it for ads, profiling, or anything else.

03

Storage

Your data is stored in Supabase. We do not sell your information or share it with third parties.

04

Chrome extension

The Iris Chrome extension reads Canvas data using your existing browser session. We never store your Canvas password.

05

Deleting your data
